# Founder Skills Research

## YC Co-Founder Matching Platform - 5 Core Skill Areas

Based on YC's platform with 16,000+ profiles:

1. **Product** - 65% of founders do this
2. **Design** - 37% of founders do this  
3. **Engineering** - 31% of founders do this (but 62% want a co-founder who does this!)
4. **Sales & Marketing** - 52% of founders do this
5. **Operations** - 57% of founders do this

## What Founders Want Most

- **Engineering is in highest demand**: 80% of non-technical founders want an Engineering co-founder
- Even engineers want more engineers: 44% of Engineering founders prefer another Engineering co-founder
- Engineers also seek complementary skills: 74% want Sales & Marketing, 53% want Operations
